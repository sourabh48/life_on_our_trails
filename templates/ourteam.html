About Us

Our Story

The Pandemic Lockdown supported new ways on the digital platforms for everybody. Occasionally somebody would be either looking for data or contributing to a blog at some point of time.

Seeing this we concocted the plan to make a stage where our users can discover data on every point given by our Bloggers, All in One Place.

Likewise, this will make a local area of bloggers where they can develop and uphold one another by sharing their day to day story of their life through various contents. From this thought, we concocted the name "The Life on The Trails".

Our Mission

Our main goal is to carry data to our perusers given by our Bloggers and keep them refreshed on every point.

Additionally to make a local area of bloggers, from where they can develop their compass, worth, and experience just as help and backing one another.
